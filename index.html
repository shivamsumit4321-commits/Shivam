Library Code of Conduct

Rules & Regulations

Please follow these guidelines to keep Sharda Library a respectful space for everyone.

Strict Silence Zone

Maintain absolute silence at all times. No talking, whispering, or phone calls inside.

Silent Mode Mandatory

All phones must be on silent/vibrate before entering the library premises.

ID Card Required

Show your library ID or booking confirmation at the reception before taking your seat.

No Food or Beverages

No eating or drinking (except sealed water bottles) inside the study area.

Punctuality Matters

Arrive on time. Seats unused for 30 minutes may be given to walk-in students.

Keep Workspace Clean

Leave your seat tidy. Don't leave wrappers, papers or personal items behind.

No Seat Sharing

One seat, one member only. Sharing ID or seat is not permitted.

Personal Belongings

Library is not responsible for lost items. Use the locker for valuable belongings.

Important: Rule violations may result in membership cancellation without refund. CCTV is active 24×7 on all premises.
